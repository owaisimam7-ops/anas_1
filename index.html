About SplashCircle.Co

SplashCircle.Co makes student life easier by building a bridge between newcomers and experienced seniors.

New students often feel confused in their early university days. They may need guidance about campus life, academic environment, routines, and general support. SplashCircle.Co solves this by offering a digital platform where newcomers can connect with seniors and get practical help.


The idea starts from UOW campus, but the vision is bigger. In the future, SplashCircle.Co can grow into a broader student support network for other universities and communities as well.

How It Works

SplashCircle.Co keeps the student support journey simple and useful.

1. New Student Visits

The student opens the website and explores support options.

2. Chooses a Senior

The student checks mentor cards and contacts a senior through WhatsApp or email.

3. Sends a Request

The student fills the help form to explain what support is needed.

4. Gets Guided

The student receives useful campus guidance and settles in more confidently.
