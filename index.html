About SplashCircle.Co

SplashCircle.Co is a digital student support platform built to make university life easier for new students. It begins with UOW campus and aims to grow into a wider student support network across more universities and locations.

Many newcomers feel confused when they enter university for the first time. They often need help with campus life, routines, guidance, social adjustment, and understanding how everything works. SplashCircle.Co solves this by creating a digital platform where students can connect with experienced seniors, ask questions, and receive useful support.


Our platform is not only about communication. It is about building a trusted ecosystem for students where guidance, mentorship, and student experience come together in one place. This helps newcomers feel more comfortable, more informed, and more connected from the start.

How It Works

SplashCircle.Co keeps the process simple, student-friendly, and easy to grow.

1. Students Visit the Platform

New students access SplashCircle.Co to learn about support options and explore student guidance.

2. They Connect With Seniors

Experienced senior students become the first point of support for newcomers through guidance and communication.

3. They Receive Practical Help

Students get assistance related to university life, adjustment, and campus understanding in a more direct way.

4. The Community Expands

As the number of students grows, SplashCircle.Co becomes a larger digital student ecosystem across campuses.
